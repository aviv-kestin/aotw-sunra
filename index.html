The World of Sun Ra

Composer, leader and renowed afrofuturist Sun Ra's place in the jazz idiom is becoming more established, but many still aren't aware of him or his unique contributions to music as a whole.

Born Herman Blount in 1914, Sun Ra was a boundary breaker and experimentalist from the beginning of his music career. His musical accomplishments cannot be overlooked, being an early adopter of the synthesizer in the 1950s, blending jazz traditions and unique experimentation through his orchestra, known as the Arkestra.
